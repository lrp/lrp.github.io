About Me

Currently a member of the Caltech LIGO Laboratory Astrophysics group, where I work on the detection and characterization of ripples in spacetime created by colliding black holes in distant galaxies. Through my career in gravitational wave astrophysics, I have developed a firm foundation in theory, source simulation, experimental design, statistics, programming, and data science. I am eager to use these fundamental skills to solve a variety of problems in different fields.
